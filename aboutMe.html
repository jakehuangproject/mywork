About

My name is Jake Huang. I am pursuing a career in UI/Ux design and interior design. I have a great passion for creating meaningful and efficient digital experiences for users. I specialize in building usable, legible, and professional websites. My knowledge of HTML, CSS, and Javascript allow me to streamline the process of designing digital products.

I am a self-motivated individual who thrives on personal and professional growth. I am a quick learner who thinks strategically. My empathic skills enable me to solve a variety of problems by understanding other people’s perspectives.

In my own time, I like to take portraits, landscapes and dog photos with my friends. I love drawing, too. I always imagine that there would be a day I could combine my photography , drawing, design skills to build a new form of art.
