Brave Story

The project was a game that raised a critical topic which could be political, social, environmental and etc. My critical topic for this project was focused on wildfires in California and their deadly nature. I planned to highlight their destructive ability and how their annual occurrences are unsustainable.

Tools Used: Adobe Illustrator & Processing
Product Type: Gaming Design & Interface Design


Research and Planning

Wildfires are quickly becoming a yearly environmental threat in California. According to The Insurance Information Institute’s article on wildfires, from the years 2017-2018 a total of nearly 20 million acres were burned as a result of wildfires.

The deadliest fire ever recorded in California, The Camp Fire of 2018 is estimated to have totaled up to $10.5 billion in insured losses and caused the death of 86 people. After doing the research about wildfires, I decided to create a game relative to this topic.


Development Process



Prototype & Aesthetics

I would like to use the different color templates to convey that wildfire affects the environment in different degrees. Furthermore, I would like to use the game to convey a piece of information that people should not go to somewhere like forest in the dry weather.

Lessons Learned

Because it was my first project that needed to make a code for a game, I experienced a lot of coding problems. I learned that the brackets had to indent properly. When I made a lot of If statements inside of if statements, if I didn’t organize these brackets, there would occur logical problems which could lead partial of code ineffective. Furthermore, if I didn’t organize the code, my teammate would have a difficult time to read the code.

Another thing that I learned was not to alway ask the professor and let the professor solve the problem. I tried to think out of the box, and I started to find what problems my code had, where the problems are, and thought about how I could solve them. I found that I could use the console in Processing to check which part of code was not working, and then found a solution step by step. Overall, solving coding problems was difficult, but the learning process promoted me a lot on problem solving.
